The March 2026 newsletter opened with a welcome to new owners and tenants and a note of appreciation to current residents.

## Welcome to new owners and tenants

The estate welcomed new owners and tenants and noted that each new household becomes part of the wider Lombardy community.

## Thank you to existing residents

Current residents were thanked for helping maintain estate standards through considerate conduct and care for shared spaces.

## Shared amenities remain part of everyday life

Residents were reminded that the clubhouse may be booked for small events through estate management and remains part of the estate’s everyday amenity offering.

## Governance context

The newsletter also highlighted the role of the Community Schemes Ombud Service (CSOS) in community scheme governance, rule oversight, and dispute processes.
